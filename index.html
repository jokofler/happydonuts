Specifications

Happy Donuts is a collection of 10.000 randomly auto-generated Donuts who are turning the world into a sweeter place by providing love and a family place for everyone who wants to join.

Each Donut is unique and generated from over 140 possible traits, including, frozings, toppings, eyes, mouths, skills and much much more.

The price per Donut is 2 Solana

Have a Question?

How do I buy a Happy Donut?

You will need a web3 wallet (example: Phantom) with Solana funds, and you will connect it to our official website: https://happydonuts.io/, connect your wallet and mint the NFT token. It will require a transaction to happen.

What is so special about happy Donuts?

Happy Donuts is a project which took us 8 months to prepare for. We have build a truly amazing team with deeply embedded values. Happy Donuts will not just be a Hype but a culture shift. Bringing back the Art of Joy.

How many will I be able to mint?

20 NFTs per transaction.

How do I know if this is the right project for me?

You never know. I guess look at the art, look at the values, look at the team and where we are heading. If you feel like enjoying life is something you want to aswell then you should be with us :-).

Does Happy Donuts have a rarity?

Yes, depending on the combination of your traits, your NFT may be more rare than others, we will provide a tool to make it easy to see and compare.
